Typing Matrices is a speed typing test platform that helps users measure and improve their typing speed, accuracy, and overall performance through structured levels and real-time analytics.

From home row basics to advanced paragraph challenges, our 60-level system adapts to your skill level and pushes you to constantly improve and grow.
